How does it work?

ODI is a type of disability insurance that pays out if the insured is unable to work in a job that they are qualified for based on their education, experience, and age. Any occupation policy would not pay benefits if the insured could still work, even if it was at a lower-paying job. If you work in an injury-prone profession, you should definitely invest in disability insurance. Disability insurance is like life insurance. What is Principle Of Solidarity?

The principle of solidarity is a socioethical and political concept that states that it is fair and just for members of society to share benefits and obligations equally. What ODI Covers ?

There are a few specific conditions to be met upon beginning the insurance cover or in the event of an extension of the insurance cover.
